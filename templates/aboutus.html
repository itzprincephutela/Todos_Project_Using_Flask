About Us

Welcome to our Todo application. This application allows you to manage your tasks efficiently by adding, updating, and deleting todos. We aim to help you stay organized and productive.

This app was developed using the Flask framework and SQLAlchemy for database management. Below are the main features:

  • Add new todos with a title and description.
  • Update existing todos to keep information current.
  • Delete todos when they are no longer needed.
  • View a list of all your todos in a structured format.

Thank you for using our application!
